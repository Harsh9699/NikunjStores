How It Began

Rooted in Radhavallabh Sampradaya

Nikunj Stores was born from a simple longing — to make sure every devotee, near or far, could dress and adorn their Thakur ji with the same love shown in Vrindavan and Barsana. What started as a small offering among friends grew into a curated collection of poshaks, idols, malas, and attars, each sourced with the sanctity of seva in mind.

We work hand-in-hand with artisan families who have stitched poshaks and strung malas for generations, ensuring every product that reaches you carries both craftsmanship and blessing.
